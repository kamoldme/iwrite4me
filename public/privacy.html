Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use iWrite4.me, we collect:

  • Account information: Your name and email address (provided via Google Sign-In).
  • Writing data: Documents you create, word counts, session durations, and XP earned.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and general interaction patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the iWrite4.me service.
  • Track your writing progress, streaks, levels, and achievements.
  • Enable social features (friends, duels, leaderboards).
  • Improve the service and fix bugs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your writing content is private by default. When you share a document, only people with the share link can access it. Leaderboard data (name, word count, level) is visible to other users.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers hosted by Railway. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords.

5. Google Sign-In

We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. We only access your basic profile information (name and email). We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or other Google services.

6. Cookies

We use localStorage to store your authentication token. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete individual documents from within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us through the in-app support system.

8. Children's Privacy

iWrite4.me is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, use the in-app support system or email us at support@iwrite4.me.
